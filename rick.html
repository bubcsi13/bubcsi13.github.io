Rick and Morty's house is a typical suburban home located in an unknown part of the multiverse. The exterior is unremarkable, with a blue roof and white walls, but the interior is a different story. Rick, a mad scientist, has turned the basement into a high-tech laboratory filled with dangerous and unpredictable devices and gadgets. The living room is cluttered with Rick's inventions and experiments, and there are portals to other dimensions scattered throughout the house. Despite its chaos and danger, Rick and Morty's house is a place of laughter, love, and adventure. It is the perfect reflection of the chaotic and unpredictable nature of Rick and Morty's lives.
